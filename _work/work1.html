Find My Bus is one of the UK's first live bus tracking apps. The aim is to give bus passengers a better idea of when their bus will be at their stop by using GPS and bus route data combined to provide a more connected experience. My role as lead designer included creating brand guidelines and marketing material, designing the app's user interface and user experience for the desktop, mobile site and mobile app. I developed the prototype app architecture and interface including the look, user flows and notifications, including sounds and haptic feedback.
